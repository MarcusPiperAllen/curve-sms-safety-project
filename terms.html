Curve Community Alerts

1. Overview

Curve Community Alerts is an SMS notification service that sends safety alerts, maintenance updates, and community notices on behalf of apartment communities and property managers.

Important: This service is not an emergency service. In case of an emergency, please call 911 or your local emergency number immediately.

2. Use of the Service

  • Residents must opt in voluntarily to receive messages, either via the web form or by texting START to the community's designated number.
  • Messages may include safety notices, maintenance updates, community announcements, and other relevant alerts.
  • Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

3. Opt-In and Opt-Out

  • To Subscribe: Fill out the web opt-in form or text START to your community's number.
  • To Unsubscribe: Text STOP at any time to opt out of all messages.
  • For Help: Text HELP to receive basic assistance and contact information.
